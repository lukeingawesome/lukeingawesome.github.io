About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at Seoul National University in Biomedical Engineering and the Integrated Major in Innovative Medical Science. My research is focused on applying advanced AI to clinical radiology, especially in the analysis of X-ray and CT images.

My interests center on:

  • Medical vision-language models (Medical VLMs)
  • Medical visual-language pretraining (Medical VLP)
  • Building reliable and robust foundation models for medical imaging
  • Clinical applications for chest and musculoskeletal imaging
I aim to bridge cutting-edge AI with real clinical needs, creating tools that are not only powerful but trustworthy for physicians.
